The Access Gap $3,500+

Current AR and VR devices are engineering marvels, but they are fundamentally flawed for emerging markets. By packing heavy batteries and expensive processors directly into the headset, they drive prices out of reach, denying African institutions easy access to the technology.

The Seer ~$100

The supercomputer required to run AR already exists in the pockets of millions: the smartphone. At Oju, we are building “The Seer”, an AR/VR device that offloads the heavy processing, battery, and internet to the phone you already own, reducing the cost of hardware by 90% without sacrificing high-fidelity immersion.

The Seer By Oju

Spatial Computing. Radically Accessible.

A beautiful lightweight pair of premium optical lenses and sensors. No heavy batteries. No cooling fans. Just pure, frictionless immersion. Our proprietary hardware architecture strips the heavy processing out of the headset, safely translating complex 3D math before it reaches the phone.

Internet bandwidth is a massive bottleneck in emerging markets. Our B2B architecture runs on local, offline Wi-Fi routers. A facility can broadcast a 3D asset locally to 50 tethered headsets simultaneously, with zero cellular data consumed.

Building the Future, Across Industries

When you radically lower the cost of entry, you change who gets to build the future.

Medical & Healthcare Parity

The Seer projects high-fidelity holograms, like a beating human respiratory system, directly onto a classroom desk, enabling risk-free training before touching real patients.

Immersive Education

Students in a single classroom can simultaneously interact with virtual field trips or practice real-world skills in safe environments, completely independent of expensive internet bandwidth.

Engineering & Architecture

Teams can physically walk around 3D CAD models, structural blueprints, and mechanical prototypes projected onto their boardroom tables, accelerating the review process.

Media & Entertainment

Event venues can run live AR shows, and cinemas can offer fully immersive VR movie experiences to hundreds of people at once. Because our system runs securely offline, filmmakers can share their content at a fraction of the usual cost, with zero risk of piracy.
